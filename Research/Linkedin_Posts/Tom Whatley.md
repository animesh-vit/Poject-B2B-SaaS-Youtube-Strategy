## Post 1

**Date:** Approx. May 2026

**Main topic:**  
SaaS YouTube visibility in AI Overviews.

**Key insights:**

- Grizzle analyzed 3,623 videos across 71 SaaS YouTube channels.
- YouTube appeared in AI Overviews for 74.9% of SaaS keywords that triggered an AIO event.
- For 542 BOFU keywords, YouTube appeared in AIOs 75.1% of the time.
- Brand-owned YouTube channels appeared for only 0.7% of 138 branded queries.
- The study also looks at title patterns, thumbnails, formats, and Shorts.

**Actionable takeaway for B2B SaaS YouTube strategy:**

- Treat YouTube as a serious AI-search visibility channel.
- Optimize videos for BOFU queries, branded queries, and buyer evaluation terms.
- Improve titles, thumbnails, and formats so brand-owned videos can show up where buyers and AI systems look.

## Post 2

**Date:** Approx. May 2026

**Main topic:**  
Balancing SEO and GEO with original expertise.

**Key insights:**

- Google AI Mode is expected to surface perspectives from communities, social media, and firsthand sources.
- Generic content is less defensible as AI search grows.
- B2B content needs to answer granular audience questions and jobs-to-be-done.
- Credible experience, original stories, and expert perspectives matter more.
- Brands still need both traditional SEO and AI-search/GEO content strategies.

**Actionable takeaway for B2B SaaS strategy:**

- Build content from real customer problems, expert experience, and unique stories.
- Keep traditional SEO foundations while adding GEO-ready content for AI search surfaces.
- Create content that answers specific buyer questions better than commodity search results.
