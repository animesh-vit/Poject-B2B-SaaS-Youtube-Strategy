## Post 1

**Date:** 4 months ago

**Main topic:**  
Cybersecurity marketing mistakes that lose prospects.

**Key insights:**

- Cybersecurity buyers reject vague, hype-driven messaging.
- AI claims need transparency, not magic-language positioning.
- Messaging should address resilience, alert fatigue, Zero Trust, and real buyer pain.
- Content should include customer voices, thought leadership, trusted research, and multi-channel distribution.
- Targeting only the CISO misses other important buyers like IT Ops, Compliance, and business stakeholders.

**Actionable takeaway for B2B SaaS YouTube strategy:**

- Create cybersecurity videos that are specific, proof-backed, and buyer-aware.
- Feature customers, researchers, and practitioners instead of only internal speakers.
- Build YouTube topics around real security concerns: resilience, alert fatigue, identity, AI transparency, and compliance.
- Use YouTube as part of a multi-channel campaign with newsletters, native articles, trusted research, and creator partnerships.
